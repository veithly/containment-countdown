## Demo Chapter Lines

Use these only if the video is muted.

1. "First frame: the judge sees the exact promise."
2. "The threshold moves, and the evidence changes state."
3. "The operator approves. The system does not self-contain."
4. "The dossier records signal, context, decision, replay action, and proof."
5. "The architecture states the boundary: seeded Splunk-compatible replay, live Cloudflare storage, live reasoning route."

## Things Not To Say

- Do not say live Splunk is connected.
- Do not present the reasoning route as the approver.
- Do not say this performs real IAM or firewall remediation.
- Do not explain Cloudflare before the judge has seen the state change.
